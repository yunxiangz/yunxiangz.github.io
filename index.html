[Publications] [Honors] [Services]



I obtained the PhD from School of Computer Science and Information System at the University of Melbourne, under the supervision of Prof. Rui Zhang and Prof. Jianzhong Qi. Before that, I graduated from Northeastern University (NEU) with the B.Eng degree from College of Software, then obtained the M.Eng degree from the National University of Defence Technology (NUDT), under the supervision of Prof. Xicheng Lu and Prof. Dongsheng Li.

My main research interests include bioinformatics, spatial data analysis, and machine learning. Specifically, I am currently doing research in bioinformatics (infectious disease transmission prediction; peptide and molecule drug discovery; vaccine design) with data mining and deep learning approaches.
