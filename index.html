I am an assistant professor at adfa (中文) in 2022.

I obtained the PhD from School of Computer Science and Information System at the University of Melbourne in 2022. Before this, I graduated from Northeastern University (NEU) for B.Eng in College of Software in 2014, then obtained the M.Eng from National University of Defense Technology (NUDT) in 2016.

My main research topics are spatial data analysis and bioinformatics. Specifically, I am currently doing research works in bioinformatices with spatial data anlaysis with deep learning approaches.
