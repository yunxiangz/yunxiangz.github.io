I am an assistant professor at Beijing Institute of Biotechnology ( ).

I obtained the PhD from School of Computer Science and Information System at the University of Melbourne in 2022.
Before this, I graduated from Northeastern University (NEU) for B.Eng in College of Software in 2014, then obtained the M.Eng from National University of Defense Technology (NUDT) in 2016.

My main research topics are bioinformatics, spatial data analysis, and machine learning. Specifically, I am currently doing research works in bioinformatices with spatial data mining and deep learning approaches.
